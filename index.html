Gray Area

PostCard Photography booklet for PHOTO 120 March 6, 2021

Artist Statment:

A set of night photographs that captures the in-between light and dark; the gray area. Everyone has their own definition of the Gray Area, for me, this book is a combination of Ku (The Void) and fading light with complete darkness that shows the viewer an undefined area of screaming and silence. The poem from the book is by Dylan Thomas; “Do not go gentle into that good night”. All pictures are taking in San Francisco Bay area.
